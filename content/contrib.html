Want to Contribute?

Contribute however you can! We need help with everything:

Just Show Up: the best way to make CryptoParty ATX a success is by showing up to the parties. As long as people keep showing up, we'll keep having them.

Tell Your Friends: tell your friends how much fun you had and how much you learned, and encourage them to join in the fun as well.

Help Plan the Next One: if you are interested in helping to plan the next CryptoParty, please contact me at plexiglass@riseup.net and I will let you know when the next meeting is, our check our ">events page.

Bring snacks/drinks: even if you can't make it to a planning meeting, feel free to bring snacks and drinks to help make the party a success. Beer, soda, and pizza are highly encouraged.

Improve This Site: Can you make this site better? Are we doing something wrong? This site is developed on github, and we welcome any contributions you may have. If you don't use github, feel free to contact plexiglass@riseup.net with any suggestions. We would love to hear from you.
